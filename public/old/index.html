About Me

I’m Hakiza Ronald, a Ugandan entrepreneur

I am Hakiza Ronald, a Ugandan entrepreneur passionate about leveraging technology to solve real-world problems. In 2015, I founded Ugabus.com, an online bus ticketing marketplace simplifying East African travel. Following a successful acquisition by Treepz, I served as Country Manager, after working as Country Launcher. Previously, I gained valuable NGO experience implementing local solutions in rural Uganda with Straight Talk Foundation. With a track record of delivering exceptional results at both tactical and executive levels. Let’s collaborate to create outstanding solutions for Africa’s pressing needs.
